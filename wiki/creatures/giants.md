## Overview

The **Giants** were born from the blood of [Uranus](../deities/uranus.md) that fell upon [Gaia](../deities/gaia.md) during his castration by [Cronos](../deities/cronos.md), alongside the [Erinyes](erinyes.md) and the Meliae nymphs.

## Description

They are described as "the great Giants with gleaming armour, holding long spears in their hands."[^theog-giants]

[^theog-giants]: "the great Giants with gleaming armour, holding long spears in their hands" (Theogony 185-186, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
