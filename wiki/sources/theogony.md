## Overview

The **Theogony** ("Birth of the Gods") is a poem by [Hesiod](works-and-days.md) that narrates the creation of the world and the genealogy of the gods, from the first primordial beings through the rise of [Zeus](../deities/zeus.md) to supremacy. It is the earliest systematic account of Greek cosmogony and divine succession.
