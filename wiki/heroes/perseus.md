## Overview

**Perseus** is a hero who slew the Gorgon [Medusa](../creatures/gorgons.md).

## Mythology

"When Perseus cut off her head, there sprang forth great Chrysaor and the horse [Pegasus](../creatures/pegasus.md) who is so called because he was born near the springs (*pegae*) of Ocean."[^theog-medusa]

[^theog-medusa]: "And when Perseus cut off her head, there sprang forth great Chrysaor and the horse Pegasus who is so called because he was born near the springs (pegae) of Ocean; and that other, because he held a golden blade (aor) in his hands." (Theogony 280-283, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
