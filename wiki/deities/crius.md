## Overview

**Crius** is one of the [Titans](titans.md).

## Birth

Listed among the children of [Gaia](gaia.md) and [Uranus](uranus.md): "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling [Oceanus](oceanus.md), [Coeus](coeus.md) and Crius and [Hyperion](hyperion.md) and [Iapetus](iapetus.md), [Theia](theia.md) and [Rhea](rhea.md), [Themis](themis.md) and [Mnemosyne](mnemosyne.md) and gold-crowned [Phoebe](phoebe.md) and lovely [Tethys](tethys.md)."[^theog-birth]

[^theog-birth]: "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling Oceanus, Coeus and Crius and Hyperion and Iapetus, Theia and Rhea, Themis and Mnemosyne and gold-crowned Phoebe and lovely Tethys" (Theogony 132-136, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Children

"Eurybia, bright goddess, was joined in love to Crius and bare great Astraeus, and Pallas, and Perses who also was eminent among all men in wisdom."[^theog-children]

[^theog-children]: "Eurybia, bright goddess, was joined in love to Crius and bare great Astraeus, and Pallas, and Perses who also was eminent among all men in wisdom" (Theogony 375-377, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Appearances

- **[Theogony](../sources/theogony.md)** 134, 375-377: Birth; children with Eurybia
- **[Bibliotheca](../sources/apollodorus.md)** 1.1.3, 1.2.2: Listed among the Titans; with Eurybia "daughter of Sea (Pontus)" bore Astraeus, Pallas, and Perses
