## Overview

**Theia** is one of the [Titans](titans.md), mother of the celestial lights.

## Birth

Listed among the children of [Gaia](gaia.md) and [Uranus](uranus.md): "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling [Oceanus](oceanus.md), [Coeus](coeus.md) and [Crius](crius.md) and [Hyperion](hyperion.md) and [Iapetus](iapetus.md), Theia and [Rhea](rhea.md), [Themis](themis.md) and [Mnemosyne](mnemosyne.md) and gold-crowned [Phoebe](phoebe.md) and lovely [Tethys](tethys.md)."[^theog-birth]

[^theog-birth]: "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling Oceanus, Coeus and Crius and Hyperion and Iapetus, Theia and Rhea, Themis and Mnemosyne and gold-crowned Phoebe and lovely Tethys" (Theogony 132-136, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Children

"Theia was subject in love to [Hyperion](hyperion.md) and bare great [Helius](helius.md) (Sun) and clear [Selene](selene.md) (Moon) and [Eos](eos.md) (Dawn) who shines upon all that are on earth and upon the deathless Gods who live in the wide heaven."[^theog-children]

[^theog-children]: "Theia was subject in love to Hyperion and bare great Helius (Sun) and clear Selene (Moon) and Eos (Dawn) who shines upon all that are on earth and upon the deathless Gods who live in the wide heaven" (Theogony 371-374, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Appearances

- **[Theogony](../sources/theogony.md)** 135, 371-374: Birth; children with Hyperion
- **[Bibliotheca](../sources/apollodorus.md)** 1.1.3, 1.2.2: Listed among the Titanides (as "Thia"); with [Hyperion](hyperion.md) bore Dawn, Sun, and Moon
