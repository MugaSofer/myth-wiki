## Overview

**Pontus** (Sea) was born of [Gaia](gaia.md) alone, "without sweet union of love." He is described as "the fruitless deep with his raging swell."[^theog-pontus]

[^theog-pontus]: "She bare also the fruitless deep with his raging swell, Pontus, without sweet union of love" (Theogony 131-132, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Children

With [Gaia](gaia.md), Pontus fathered:[^theog-children]
- [Nereus](nereus.md) — "the eldest of his children"
- Thaumas ("great")
- Phorcys ("proud")
- Ceto ("fair-cheeked")
- Eurybia — "who has a heart of flint within her"

[^theog-children]: "Sea begat Nereus, the eldest of his children... And yet again he got great Thaumas and proud Phorcys, being mated with Earth, and fair-cheeked Ceto and Eurybia who has a heart of flint within her" (Theogony 233-239)
