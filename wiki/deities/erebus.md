## Overview

**Erebus** is a primordial deity of darkness, born from [Chaos](chaos.md) alongside [Night](night.md).

## Children

From the union of Erebus and [Night](night.md) "were born Aether and Day, whom she conceived and bare from union in love with Erebus."[^theog-children]

[^theog-children]: "of Night were born Aether and Day, whom she conceived and bare from union in love with Erebus" (Theogony 124-125, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
