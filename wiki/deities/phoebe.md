## Overview

**Phoebe** is one of the [Titans](titans.md), mother of Leto and grandmother of [Apollo](apollo.md) and [Artemis](artemis.md).

## Birth

Listed among the children of [Gaia](gaia.md) and [Uranus](uranus.md): "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling [Oceanus](oceanus.md), [Coeus](coeus.md) and [Crius](crius.md) and [Hyperion](hyperion.md) and [Iapetus](iapetus.md), [Theia](theia.md) and [Rhea](rhea.md), [Themis](themis.md) and [Mnemosyne](mnemosyne.md) and gold-crowned Phoebe and lovely [Tethys](tethys.md)."[^theog-birth]

[^theog-birth]: "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling Oceanus, Coeus and Crius and Hyperion and Iapetus, Theia and Rhea, Themis and Mnemosyne and gold-crowned Phoebe and lovely Tethys" (Theogony 132-136, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Children

"Phoebe came to the desired embrace of [Coeus](coeus.md)." She is the mother of Leto (who bore [Apollo](apollo.md) and [Artemis](artemis.md)) and Asteria.[^theog-children]

[^theog-children]: "Again, Phoebe came to the desired embrace of Coeus" (Theogony 404, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
