## Overview

**Tethys** is one of the [Titans](titans.md), consort of [Oceanus](oceanus.md) and mother of the rivers and Oceanids.

## Birth

Listed among the children of [Gaia](gaia.md) and [Uranus](uranus.md): "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling [Oceanus](oceanus.md), [Coeus](coeus.md) and [Crius](crius.md) and [Hyperion](hyperion.md) and [Iapetus](iapetus.md), [Theia](theia.md) and [Rhea](rhea.md), [Themis](themis.md) and [Mnemosyne](mnemosyne.md) and gold-crowned [Phoebe](phoebe.md) and lovely Tethys."[^theog-birth]

[^theog-birth]: "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling Oceanus, Coeus and Crius and Hyperion and Iapetus, Theia and Rhea, Themis and Mnemosyne and gold-crowned Phoebe and lovely Tethys" (Theogony 132-136, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Children

"Tethys bare to [Ocean](oceanus.md) eddying rivers" including Nilus, Alpheus, Eridanus, and many more. She also bore three thousand Oceanid daughters.[^theog-children]

[^theog-children]: "Tethys bare to Ocean eddying rivers, Nilus, and Alpheus, and deep-swirling Eridanus, Strymon, and Meander..." and "there are three thousand neat-ankled daughters of Ocean who are dispersed far and wide" (Theogony 334-370, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
