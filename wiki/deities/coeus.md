## Overview

**Coeus** is one of the [Titans](titans.md), consort of [Phoebe](phoebe.md) and grandfather of [Apollo](apollo.md) and [Artemis](artemis.md).

## Birth

Listed among the children of [Gaia](gaia.md) and [Uranus](uranus.md): "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling [Oceanus](oceanus.md), Coeus and [Crius](crius.md) and [Hyperion](hyperion.md) and [Iapetus](iapetus.md), [Theia](theia.md) and [Rhea](rhea.md), [Themis](themis.md) and [Mnemosyne](mnemosyne.md) and gold-crowned [Phoebe](phoebe.md) and lovely [Tethys](tethys.md)."[^theog-birth]

[^theog-birth]: "she lay with Heaven and bare deep-swirling Oceanus, Coeus and Crius and Hyperion and Iapetus, Theia and Rhea, Themis and Mnemosyne and gold-crowned Phoebe and lovely Tethys" (Theogony 132-136, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)

## Children

"[Phoebe](phoebe.md) came to the desired embrace of Coeus." Their children are described in Phoebe's entry.[^theog-union]

[^theog-union]: "Again, Phoebe came to the desired embrace of Coeus" (Theogony 404, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
