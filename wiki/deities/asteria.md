## Overview

**Asteria** is a daughter of [Coeus](coeus.md) and [Phoebe](phoebe.md), sister of [Leto](leto.md), and mother of [Hecate](hecate.md) by Perses.

## Mythology

"Also she bare Asteria of happy name, whom Perses once led to his great house to be called his dear wife. And she conceived and bare [Hecate](hecate.md) whom [Zeus](zeus.md) the son of Cronos honoured above all."[^theog-asteria]
