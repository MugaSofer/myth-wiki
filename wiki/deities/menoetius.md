## Overview

**Menoetius** is a son of [Iapetus](iapetus.md), struck down by [Zeus](zeus.md) for his pride.

## Mythology

"She [[Iapetus](iapetus.md)'s wife Clymene] bare him a stout-hearted son, [Atlas](atlas.md): also she bare very glorious Menoetius and clever [Prometheus](prometheus.md), full of various wiles, and scatter-brained [Epimetheus](epimetheus.md)."[^theog-birth] "But Menoetius was outrageous, and far-seeing [Zeus](zeus.md) struck him with a lurid thunderbolt and sent him down to [Erebus](erebus.md) because of his mad presumption and exceeding pride."[^theog-fate]

[^theog-birth]: "she bare him a stout-hearted son, Atlas: also she bare very glorious Menoetius and clever Prometheus, full of various wiles, and scatter-brained Epimetheus" (Theogony 509-511, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
[^theog-fate]: "But Menoetius was outrageous, and far-seeing Zeus struck him with a lurid thunderbolt and sent him down to Erebus because of his mad presumption and exceeding pride" (Theogony 514-516, trans. H.G. Evelyn-White)
