## Overview

The **Olympians** are the major gods who dwell on [Mount Olympus](../../places/olympus.md) and rule the cosmos after the defeat of the [Titans](titans.md) in the [Titanomachy](../../events/titanomachy.md). The canonical count is twelve, though ancient sources vary on exact membership.

## Members

The core Olympians as attested across multiple sources:

1. [Zeus](../zeus.md) — king of the gods; sky and thunder
2. [Hera](../hera.md) — queen of the gods; marriage
3. [Poseidon](../poseidon.md) — sea and earthquakes
4. [Athena](../athena.md) — wisdom, warfare, and crafts
5. [Apollo](../apollo.md) — archery, music, prophecy, plague
6. [Artemis](../artemis.md) — hunting, wild animals, the moon
7. [Ares](../ares.md) — war
8. [Aphrodite](../aphrodite.md) — love and beauty
9. [Hermes](../hermes.md) — messengers, travellers, thieves
10. [Hephaestus](../hephaestus.md) — fire and metalwork
11. [Demeter](../demeter.md) — grain and harvest
12. [Dionysus](../dionysus.md) — wine and ecstasy

### Variable Members

- [Hestia](../hestia.md) — hearth; sometimes counted among the twelve in place of Dionysus
- [Hades](../hades.md) — lord of the underworld; shares the three-way division of the cosmos with Zeus and Poseidon but dwells beneath the earth rather than on Olympus

### Other Olympus-Dwelling Deities

Several deities reside on or frequent Olympus without traditionally being counted among the twelve:

- [Hebe](../hebe.md) — cupbearer of the gods
- [Eileithyia](../eileithyia.md) — goddess of childbirth
- [Iris](../iris.md) — divine messenger
- [Asclepius](../asclepius.md) — healing (deified)
- The [Muses](muses.md) — song and inspiration
- The [Graces](graces.md) (Charites) — beauty and charm
- The [Horae](horae.md) (Hours) — order and the seasons
